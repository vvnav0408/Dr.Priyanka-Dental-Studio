What We Do

Comprehensive Dental Services

We combine advanced technology with precision techniques to deliver long-lasting, beautiful smile health for you and your family.

Root Canal Treatment

Relieve pain and save your natural teeth with precise, gentle, and modern root canal therapies.

Laser Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile safely and effectively in a single session with our medical-grade dental lasers.

Gum Therapy

Expert periodontal treatments to combat bleeding gums, bone loss, and secure your teeth's foundations.

Invisible Aligners & Braces

Dr. Remya Mohanan, B.D.S.

Chief Dental Surgeon & Cosmetic Specialist

Dr. Remya Mohanan is a highly skilled dental surgeon dedicated to providing precise dental therapies with a compassionate touch. As the chief dentist of Smile Solutions Multi Speciality Dental Clinic, she focuses on diagnosing and treating various dental issues, executing modern procedures, and restoring functional smiles.
