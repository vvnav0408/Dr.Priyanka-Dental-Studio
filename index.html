Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Priyanka Menon, B.D.S., M.D.S.

Periodontist, Oral Implantologist and Laser Specialist

Dr. Priyanka Menon is a highly skilled dental specialist dedicated to providing precise dental therapies with a compassionate touch. As an MDS in Periodontics, an Oral Implantologist, and a certified Laser Specialist, she focuses on diagnosing and treating complex gum diseases, executing suture-less laser treatments, and restoring functional smiles using advanced dental implants.

At Dr. Priyanka's Dental Studio, patient comfort and state-of-the-art precision are at the heart of every procedure. Dr. Priyanka believes in personalized dental care, working closely with every patient to craft tailored treatment paths that promote overall wellness and deliver lasting, radiant smiles.
